## About

The concept of algorithmic problem-solving can be traced back to ancient times, long before the invention of computers. In fact, they are as old as problem-solving itself. In simple words, algorithms are used to arrive at a solution of a complex problem by breaking down the problem-solving journey into simple sequence of steps. Possessing a good algorithm before writing a computer program is as important as having a clear and precise set of cooking instructions before attempting to make a dish. Algorithmic thinking is an essential skill for a computer programmer. It is even more fundamental than programming skills.
